Lomo Saltado Recipe

Lomo Saltado: a Peruvian Dish

Lomo Saltadois a widely known Spanish, Latin American, dish with many variations and recipes depending on who you ask. This recipe is simple to prep and cook and is a perfect easy dish that you can make with spare ingredients around the house!

Ingredients you will need:

  • 1 lb Steak(a tender loin is preffered)
  • 2 Bell peppers
  • 1 cup White rice
  • 2-3 Garlic cloves
  • 1/2-1 Red Onion
  • 1 bag of Cut Fries, any cut your choice
  • 1 bundle Cilantro (optional for garnish)

How to make:

  1. To start, cut your beef into thinner strips if not already so. Season your steak.
  2. Heat a medium pan to a med-high heat with a tsp of an oil of your choice.
  3. Throw the meat into the ban and begin to cook for 5 min or until brown.
  4. Cut your vegetables while the meat is cooking.
  5. Once the meat is done, remove from pan and toss in your peppers and onions
  6. Cook for 3 min or until soft, then add garlic.
